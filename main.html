Kokan


The Konkan extends throughout the western coasts of Maharashtra, Goa and Karnataka. It is bounded by the Western Ghats mountain range (also known as Sahyadri) in the east, the Arabian Sea in the west, the Daman Ganga River in the north and the River Aghanashini in the south

  2. Alibaug: The Best Of Everything.
  3. Ratnagiri: The Port Town.
  4. Harnai: Home To Beaches.
  5. Murud: For That Perfect Break.
